Semi-automatic sealers partially automate the sealing process, increasing efficiency and throughput. Fully automatic sealers are completely automated, requiring minimal operator involvement and are ideal for high-volume production. Each type of sealer offers different levels of automation and production capabilities to suit various needs in the medical industry.</p></p>
