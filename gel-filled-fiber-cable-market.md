<p><p>Gel filled fiber cables are available in two main market types: single-mode and multi-mode cables. Single-mode cables are designed for long-distance transmission with a smaller core diameter, allowing for a single mode of light to travel through. Multi-mode cables have a larger core diameter, allowing multiple modes of light to travel through at the same time. Each market type caters to different needs and applications, with single-mode cables being suitable for longer distances and multi-mode cables being better suited for shorter distances.</p></p>
