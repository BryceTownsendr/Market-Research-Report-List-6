<p><ul><li>Distributed</li><li>Centralized</li><li>Modular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Battery Control Unit (BCU) market can be categorized into three types: distributed, centralized, and modular. Distributed BCUs manage individual battery cells or packs, enhancing fault tolerance and balancing. Centralized BCUs oversee multiple battery units from a single control point, simplifying management but potentially creating single points of failure. Modular BCUs combine features of both, allowing for scalability and flexibility in larger systems, making them suitable for applications that require adaptability while maintaining efficient monitoring and control.</p></p>
