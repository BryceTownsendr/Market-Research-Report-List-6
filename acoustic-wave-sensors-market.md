<p><p>Acoustic wave sensors are categorized into two main types: Surface Acoustic Wave Sensors (SAW) and Bulk Acoustic Wave Sensors (BAW). SAW sensors are based on the propagation of acoustic waves along the surface of a piezoelectric material, while BAW sensors involve the propagation of waves through the bulk of the material. Both types are used for various applications such as pressure, temperature, and gas sensing due to their high sensitivity and accuracy in detecting changes in acoustic waves.</p></p>
