<p><p>The ultra-thin heat pipes market comprises two primary types: round heat pipes and flat heat pipes. Round heat pipes are cylindrical in shape, offering effective heat transfer for applications with limited space, making them ideal for compact electronic devices. Flat heat pipes, on the other hand, have a rectangular profile, enabling them to distribute heat over larger surfaces and are commonly used in modern cooling solutions for laptops and high-performance electronics. Both types enhance thermal management in various industries.</p></p>
