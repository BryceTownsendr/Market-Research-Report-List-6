<p><p>Shoulder prostheses are artificial devices used to replace a damaged or missing shoulder joint. They are commonly used in patients suffering from severe arthritis, traumatic injuries, or other conditions that affect the shoulder joint. The main components of a shoulder prosthesis include a metal stem that is inserted into the upper arm bone (humerus), a plastic socket that replaces the shoulder socket (glenoid), and a metal ball that replaces the top of the humerus.</p><p>The shoulder prostheses market is expected to grow at a steady pace, with a projected CAGR of 5.9% during the forecast period.
