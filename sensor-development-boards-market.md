<p><p>Sensor development boards play a crucial role in prototyping and testing sensor technologies. The market consists of various types: </p><p>1. **Adapter Boards** facilitate connections between different sensor types and platforms.  </p><p>2. **Demonstration Boards** showcase sensor capabilities for potential users.  </p><p>3. **Evaluation Boards** allow developers to assess performance for specific applications.  </p><p>4. **Add-On Boards** enhance existing systems with additional sensors or functionalities.  </p><p>5. **Breakout Boards** simplify connections between sensors and microcontrollers, making integration easier. Each type serves unique developmental needs in the sensor technology landscape.</p></p>
