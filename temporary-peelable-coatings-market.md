<p><p>The Temporary Peelable Coatings market is primarily categorized into solvent-based and water-based types. Solvent-based peelable coatings utilize organic solvents, providing a durable layer that offers excellent adhesion and resistance to harsh conditions, making them suitable for industrial applications. In contrast, water-based peelable coatings are formulated with water as a solvent, promoting eco-friendliness and lower VOC emissions. These coatings are easy to apply and remove, making them popular for temporary protection in various sectors, including automotive and construction.</p></p>
