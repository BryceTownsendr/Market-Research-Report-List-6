<p><p>The AR head-mounted display (HMD) optical waveguide market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Geometric Waveguide**: Uses physical structures to manipulate light, offering a simple design and effective light management.</p><p>2. **Diffractive Waveguide (Surface Relief Grating)**: Utilizes surface patterns to diffract light, allowing compact designs with high-resolution images, suitable for intricate applications.</p><p>3. **Diffractive Waveguide (Volume Holographic Gratings)**: Employs holographic techniques to achieve versatility and high image quality, ideal for immersive experiences in AR applications. Each type caters to different user needs and technological requirements.</p></p>
