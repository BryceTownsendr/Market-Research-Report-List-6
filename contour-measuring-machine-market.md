<p><p>The contour measuring machine market is divided into two primary types: contact and non-contact measuring machines. Contact measuring machines utilize physical probes to directly touch and measure the surface contours of objects, providing high precision and accuracy. In contrast, non-contact measuring machines employ optical, laser, or other sensing technologies to assess surface profiles without physical interaction, allowing for faster measurements and the ability to measure delicate or soft materials. Both types cater to various industrial applications requiring precise contour measurements.</p></p>
