<p><p>The EOOE (Electrical Opto-Optical Electrical) converters market is categorized into two main types: single mode and multimode. Single mode converters are designed for long-distance communication, utilizing a small core fiber that allows a single light mode to propagate, resulting in higher bandwidth and lower signal loss. In contrast, multimode converters support multiple light modes within a larger core fiber, making them suitable for shorter distances and lower-cost applications. Both types serve specific needs in telecommunications and data transmission systems.</p></p>
