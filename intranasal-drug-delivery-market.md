<p><p>Intranasal drug delivery is a method of administering medications through the nose into the nasal cavity. This allows for the rapid absorption of drugs into the bloodstream, bypassing the digestive system and first-pass metabolism. This route of drug administration is particularly useful for medications that have poor oral bioavailability or need to reach the brain quickly, such as treatments for migraines, allergies, and central nervous system disorders.</p><p>The Intranasal Drug Delivery Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.6% during the forecast period.
