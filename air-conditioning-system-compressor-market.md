<p><strong>The Air Conditioning System Compressor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Scroll Type Compressor</li><li>Rotary Type Compressor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air conditioning system compressor market includes two primary types: scroll type compressors and rotary type compressors. Scroll type compressors utilize two spirals to compress refrigerant, offering high efficiency and low noise, making them ideal for residential and commercial applications. On the other hand, rotary type compressors use a rotating mechanism to compress refrigerant, providing a compact design and better performance in smaller systems. Both types are essential for enhancing cooling efficiency and overall system effectiveness in air conditioning units.</p></p>
