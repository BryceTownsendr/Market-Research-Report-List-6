<p><p>The pharmaceutical blister films market includes several key types: PCTFE (polychlorotrifluoroethylene) offers excellent moisture barrier properties; PVDC (polyvinylidene chloride) provides high barrier protection against gases and moisture; COC (cyclic olefin copolymer) cold form foil ensures robust sealing and is ideal for sensitive formulations. PVC (polyvinyl chloride) is commonly used for its cost-effectiveness, while PP (polypropylene) is valued for its versatility and resistance to chemicals. Each type caters to specific packaging needs in the pharmaceutical industry, enhancing product stability and shelf life.</p></p>
