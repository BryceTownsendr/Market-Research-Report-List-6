<p><p>The agrochemical adjuvants market includes various types such as alkoxylates, sulfonates, and silicones, which enhance the efficacy of pesticides and herbicides. Alkoxylates improve the wetting and spreading of agrochemicals, while sulfonates serve as surfactants that facilitate better dispersion and stability. Silicones, known for their low surface tension, provide excellent spreading and adhesion properties. Each type plays a critical role in optimizing the performance of agrochemical formulations, ensuring more effective agricultural practices.</p></p>
