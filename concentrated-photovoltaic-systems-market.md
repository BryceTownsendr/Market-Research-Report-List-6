<p><p>Concentrated Photovoltaic Systems market consists of two main types: High Concentrated Photovoltaic (HCPV) and Low Concentrated Photovoltaic (LCPV). HCPV systems use lenses or mirrors to focus sunlight onto a small but highly efficient solar cell, allowing for higher energy production. On the other hand, LCPV systems use less intense concentration of sunlight to generate electricity. Both types have their own advantages and applications, catering to different needs and environments in the solar energy industry.</p></p>
