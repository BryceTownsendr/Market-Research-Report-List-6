<p><p>The Anti-Fingerprint Coatings market includes primarily two types: hydrophobic and oleophobic coatings. Hydrophobic coatings repel water, preventing moisture and contaminants from adhering to surfaces, making them ideal for outdoor applications and devices exposed to wet conditions. Oleophobic coatings, on the other hand, are designed to resist oils and grease, effectively repelling fingerprints and smudges from surfaces like touchscreen devices. Together, these coatings enhance the appearance and longevity of products by maintaining cleanliness and reducing maintenance needs.</p></p>
