<p><p>PCR film labels are categorized based on the type of material used, which influences their applications and environmental impact. PE type labels, crafted from recycled polyethylene, are known for their flexibility and cost-effectiveness, commonly used in packaging. PP type labels, composed of recycled polypropylene, provide excellent resistance to moisture and chemicals, making them ideal for various applications, including food packaging.</p></p>
