<p><p>Electro-fusion couplers are specialized components used primarily in the joining of plastic pipes, particularly in gas and water distribution systems. This technology utilizes electrical currents to heat and soften the thermoplastic material at the joint, enabling a secure and efficient fusion without additional adhesives or mechanical fasteners.
