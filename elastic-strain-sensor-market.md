<p><p>The Elastic Strain Sensor market comprises three main types: resistive, capacitive, and others. Resistive sensors operate based on changes in electrical resistance as they deform, making them reliable for various applications. Capacitive sensors detect variations in capacitance caused by strain, offering high sensitivity and accuracy. Other types may include piezoelectric and optical sensors, which utilize different physical principles to measure strain. Each type serves distinct applications in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and structural health monitoring.</p></p>
