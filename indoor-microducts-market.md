<p><p>The indoor microducts market is divided into thick wall and thin-walled microducts, each serving distinct purposes. Thick wall microducts offer enhanced durability and protection, making them ideal for high-density environments where physical stress is a concern. Conversely, thin-walled microducts are lightweight and flexible, suitable for applications requiring tighter bends and easier installations. Both types facilitate efficient fiber optic cable management, promoting better space utilization and minimizing installation time while maintaining optimal performance for indoor connectivity solutions.</p></p>
