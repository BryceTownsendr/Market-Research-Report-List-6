<p><p>The Beverage Plastic Bottle market consists of various types, including PP (polypropylene), PE (polyethylene), PET (polyethylene terephthalate), and others. PP bottles are known for their resistance to high temperatures and chemicals, making them suitable for hot-filled beverages. PE bottles are flexible and durable, often used for packaging juices and dairy drinks. PET bottles are lightweight and have good barrier properties, making them ideal for carbonated drinks. The "Others" category includes less common plastic bottle materials such as PVC and HDPE.</p></p>
