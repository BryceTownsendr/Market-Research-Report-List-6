<p><p>UV adhesives market consists of various types such as Acrylic, Cyanoacrylate, Epoxy, Silicone, and Polyurethane. Acrylic adhesives offer fast cure times, high transparency, and good resistance to yellowing. Cyanoacrylate adhesives provide fast bonding on a variety of substrates. Epoxy adhesives offer high strength and chemical resistance. Silicone adhesives are flexible and resistant to high temperatures. Polyurethane adhesives provide excellent impact resistance and durability. Each type of adhesive caters to different applications and industries, meeting specific requirements for bonding materials effectively.</p></p>
