<p><p>PCB waste services encompass various market types including transformer drain and flush services, disposal of PCB items and carcasses, bulk soils disposal, treatment and disposal of mixed PCB/RCRA and radioactive waste, and the PCB remediation waste market. Transformer drain and flush services involve removing PCB-contaminated oil from transformers. Disposal of PCB items and carcasses entails safely disposing of materials contaminated with PCBs. Bulk soils disposal deals with the proper disposal of soil contaminated with PCBs. Treatment and disposal of mixed PCB/RCRA and radioactive waste involves handling and disposing of hazardous waste that contains PCBs along with other regulated substances. The PCB remediation waste market focuses on managing and removing PCB-contaminated materials from sites to prevent environmental harm.</p></p>
