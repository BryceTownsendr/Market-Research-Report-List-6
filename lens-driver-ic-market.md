<p><p>Lens driver IC market is segmented into three types based on the number of channels they control: single channel, dual channel, and four channel. Single channel lens driver ICs have one channel to control the movement of one lens. Dual channel lens driver ICs have two channels to control the movement of two lenses independently. Four channel lens driver ICs have four channels to control the movement of four lenses individually. Each type caters to different requirements in applications such as cameras, smartphones, and automotive systems.</p></p>
