<p><p>Defibrillator pads market includes three types - Manual External, Manual Internal, and Automatic External. Manual External defibrillators require the user to press a button to deliver a shock, while Manual Internal defibrillators involve inserting paddles directly into the patient's chest. Automatic External defibrillators analyze the heart rhythm and administer a shock without the need for user intervention. Each type of defibrillator pad is designed for different settings and levels of user expertise, catering to a wide range of medical emergencies.</p></p>
