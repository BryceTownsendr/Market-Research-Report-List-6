<p><p>Continuous Filament Mat (CFM) is a type of nonwoven reinforcement material used in composite manufacturing. CFM is available in various forms, including Powder Mats and Emulsion Mats. Powder Mats consist of continuous filaments bonded together with powder binders, providing high mechanical strength. Emulsion Mats are similar, but use emulsion binders instead. Both types of CFM are commonly used in industries such as automotive, construction, and marine for their excellent strength, impact resistance, and corrosion protection properties.</p></p>
