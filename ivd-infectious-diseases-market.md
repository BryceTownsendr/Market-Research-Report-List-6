<p><p>The IVD Infectious Diseases market consists of two main types: Immunochemistry and Molecular Diagnostics. Immunochemistry involves the use of antibodies to detect specific antigens in a patient's sample, while Molecular Diagnostics utilizes techniques like PCR to detect the genetic material of pathogens. Immunochemistry tests are widely used for screening and monitoring infectious diseases, while Molecular Diagnostics are highly sensitive and specific, making them valuable for identifying pathogens like viruses or bacteria with great accuracy. Both types play a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring infectious diseases.</p></p>
