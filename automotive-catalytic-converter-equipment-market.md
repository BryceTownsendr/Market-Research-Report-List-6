<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive Catalytic Converter Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Three-way Catalysts (TWC)</li><li>Lean NOx Catalysts (LNC)</li><li>Lean NOx Trap (LNT)</li><li>Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR)</li><li>Diesel Oxidation Catalyst (DOC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive catalytic converter equipment market includes various types designed to reduce emissions. Three-Way Catalysts (TWC) manage CO, HC, and NOx in gasoline engines. Lean NOx Catalysts (LNC) target NOx emissions in lean-burn gasoline engines. Lean NOx Traps (LNT) capture NOx during lean operation and release it in rich conditions. Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) reduces NOx using urea in diesel engines, while Diesel Oxidation Catalysts (DOC) convert CO and hydrocarbons into carbon dioxide and water, primarily for diesel applications.</p></p>
