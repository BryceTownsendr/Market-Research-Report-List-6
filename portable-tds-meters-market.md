<p><p>Portable TDS Meters come in three different market types based on the number of channels they have - Single Channel, Dual Channel, and Multi Channel. Single Channel meters can measure the total dissolved solids in one solution at a time, while Dual Channel meters can measure two different solutions simultaneously. Multi Channel meters, on the other hand, can measure multiple solutions at the same time, making them more efficient for users who need to test multiple samples quickly and easily.</p></p>
