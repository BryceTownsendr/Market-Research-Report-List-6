<p><p>The radiation shielding materials market is categorized into three main types. Traditional lead shielding materials are widely used for their high density and effectiveness in blocking radiation, but they can be heavy and cumbersome. Lead composite shielding materials combine lead with other materials to enhance flexibility and reduce weight while maintaining protective properties. Lead-free shielding materials utilize advanced alternatives, such as barium, tungsten, or polyethylene, providing an eco-friendly option without compromising on shielding performance, catering to diverse applications and regulatory needs.</p></p>
