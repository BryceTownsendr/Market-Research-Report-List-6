HEPA filters trap 99.97% of particles 0.3 microns or larger, making them suitable for most cleanroom applications. ULPA filters offer even higher efficiency, capturing 99.999% of particles down to 0.1 microns, and are used in more stringent environments. Both types are essential for industries such as pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and electronics, ensuring contamination control.</p></p>
