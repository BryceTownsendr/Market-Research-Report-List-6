<p><p>The bio-butadiene market is categorized into two primary methods of production: one-step and multi-step methods. One-step methods typically involve direct conversion of biomass into bio-butadiene through a single catalytic process, offering efficiency and cost-effectiveness. Conversely, multi-step methods involve several stages, starting from biomass conversion to intermediates before yielding bio-butadiene, allowing for more control over the final product’s quality and composition. Both methods cater to the growing demand for sustainable alternatives to fossil-derived butadiene in various applications.</p></p>
