<p><p>Reflective Traffic Signage Films are essential for ensuring road safety by increasing visibility in various lighting conditions. There are two main types of reflective films used in traffic signage: Microprismatic and Glass Beads. Microprismatic films have tiny prisms that reflect light back to the source, making them highly visible from all angles. Glass Beads films contain microscopic glass spheres that reflect light, providing good visibility in low-light conditions. Both types of films are crucial for enhancing the visibility of traffic signs and ensuring driver safety on roads.</p></p>
