<p><p>The textile softener market encompasses various types designed to enhance fabric softness and reduce static. Dryer sheets are convenient, single-use sheets placed in dryers, providing softness while imparting pleasant fragrances. Dryer balls, typically made from wool or plastic, agitate laundry in the dryer, naturally softening fabrics and reducing drying time without chemicals.
