<p><p>Substitute Natural Gas (SNG) can be produced from various sources including coal, oil, biomass, solid waste, and others. Coal-based SNG is produced through coal gasification, while oil-based SNG is produced through the Fischer-Tropsch process. Biomass-based SNG is produced by converting organic materials into gas through anaerobic digestion or gasification. Solid waste-based SNG involves converting municipal solid waste into gas. Other sources for SNG production include industrial waste gases and biogas from wastewater treatment plants.</p></p>
