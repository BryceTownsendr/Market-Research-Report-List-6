<p><p>AC Electric Pump market refers to the market segment that includes air pumps powered by alternating current. These pumps are typically larger in size and have a higher power output compared to DC pumps. They are commonly used for inflating large inflatables such as air mattresses, pool floats, and sports equipment.</p><p>DC Electric Pump market refers to the market segment that includes air pumps powered by direct current. These pumps are more portable and convenient for on-the-go use as they can be powered by batteries or car outlets. They are often used for smaller inflatables like inflating tires, balloons, or small air mattresses.</p></p>
