<p><p>Alpha olefins are a type of unsaturated hydrocarbon with the general formula of CnH2n. The key applications of alpha olefins include polyethylene co-monomers, synthetic lubricants, and detergent alcohols.</p><p>The global alpha olefins market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
