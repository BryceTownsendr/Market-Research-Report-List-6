<p><p>The carbon black-filled nylon market is primarily categorized into PA 66 and PA 6 types. PA 66, or polyamide 66, offers enhanced thermal stability and mechanical strength, making it suitable for high-performance applications. In contrast, PA 6, or polyamide 6, is known for its excellent flexibility and impact resistance, often utilized in consumer products. Both types are enhanced with carbon black, improving properties like UV resistance, conductivity, and durability, catering to diverse industries including automotive, electronics, and textiles.</p></p>
