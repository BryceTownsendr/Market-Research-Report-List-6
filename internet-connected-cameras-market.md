<p><p>The internet-connected cameras market is primarily divided into two types: wired Wi-Fi cameras and wireless Wi-Fi cameras. Wired Wi-Fi cameras connect to a network via Ethernet cables, offering reliable connections and consistent power supply, making them ideal for surveillance in fixed locations. In contrast, wireless Wi-Fi cameras operate through Wi-Fi networks, providing flexibility and ease of installation without the clutter of cables. This makes them suitable for dynamic environments where mobility and ease of access are essential for monitoring needs.</p></p>
