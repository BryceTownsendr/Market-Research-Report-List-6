<p><p>Plastic 3D Printers market includes two main types: FDM Thermoplastic and Polyjet Photopolymer Resin. FDM printers use thermoplastic filaments which are heated and extruded layer by layer to create the desired object. On the other hand, Polyjet printers utilize liquid photopolymer resin that is cured with UV light to build the model in a layer-by-layer fashion. Both technologies offer unique advantages and applications in various industries, such as prototyping, manufacturing, and healthcare.</p></p>
