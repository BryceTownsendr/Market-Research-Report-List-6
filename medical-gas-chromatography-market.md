<p><p>The medical gas chromatography market includes two primary segments: Gas Chromatography (GC) and Gas Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry (GC-MS). GC is a technique used to separate and analyze compounds in gas form, commonly utilized for quality control and analysis in medical applications. GC-MS combines gas chromatography with mass spectrometry, allowing for comprehensive identification and quantification of complex mixtures. This enhanced capability is vital for analyzing biological samples, pharmaceuticals, and environmental substances, driving its demand in healthcare and research sectors.</p></p>
