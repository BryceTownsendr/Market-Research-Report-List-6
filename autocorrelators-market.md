<p><strong>The Autocorrelators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intensity Autocorrelators</li><li>Interferometric Autocorrelators</li><li>Scanning Autocorrelators</li><li>Single-shot Autocorrelators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Autocorrelators are devices used in optics to measure the temporal coherence of light pulses. There are four main types of autocorrelators: Intensity Autocorrelators, which measure the intensity of the pulse as a function of time; Interferometric Autocorrelators, which use interference patterns to measure the pulse width; Scanning Autocorrelators, which scan through a range of delays to measure the pulse shape; and Single-shot Autocorrelators, which capture a single shot of the pulse to provide immediate measurements. Each type caters to specific applications in the optics industry.</p></p>
