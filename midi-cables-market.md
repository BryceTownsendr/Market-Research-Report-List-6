<p><p>The MIDI cables market is primarily categorized into two types: single cables and multicore cables. Single cables typically feature a single connection, making them ideal for straightforward setups like connecting a MIDI controller to a keyboard. In contrast, multicore cables combine multiple MIDI lines into one cable, facilitating more complex systems where multiple devices need to communicate simultaneously. This versatility is essential in professional settings, such as studios and live performances, where space and organization are critical.</p></p>
