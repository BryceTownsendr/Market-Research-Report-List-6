<p><p>The EDM cutting wire market encompasses three primary types: No Coated Wire, Coated Wire, and Hybrid Wire. No Coated Wire is bare and is often used for basic applications where precision and surface finish are less critical. Coated Wire features a specialized coating that enhances its durability and performance, reducing friction and improving cut quality. Hybrid Wire combines elements of both, offering a balance between the raw strength of no coated wire and the advantages of a coating for enhanced efficiency and precision.</p></p>
