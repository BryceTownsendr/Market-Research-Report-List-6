<p><p>The shock absorbers market is segmented into hydraulic, pneumatic, and other types. Hydraulic shock absorbers use fluid to dampen vibrations, providing smooth vehicle handling. Each type serves distinct applications across automotive, industrial, and aerospace sectors, catering to varying consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
