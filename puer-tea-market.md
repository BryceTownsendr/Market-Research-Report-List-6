<p><p>Pu'er tea, a type of fermented tea originating from China, is categorized into two main market types: Pu'er raw tea and Pu'er cooked tea. Pu'er raw tea, also known as Sheng Pu'er, is produced through a natural fermentation process and is known for its fresh and lively flavors. On the other hand, Pu'er cooked tea, or Shu Pu'er, undergoes an accelerated fermentation process, resulting in a darker color and earthier taste. Both types of Pu'er tea cater to different preferences among tea connoisseurs, offering a wide range of flavors and complexities.</p></p>
