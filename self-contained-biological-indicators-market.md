Additionally, the growth of the pharmaceutical and biotechnology sectors, alongside an increasing number of surgical procedures, further propels market expansion. The rising prevalence of healthcare-associated infections (HAIs) underscores the necessity of effective sterilization processes, thereby increasing the uptake of SCBIs.
