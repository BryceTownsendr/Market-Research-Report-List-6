<p><p>Glass free 3D displays utilize two main technologies: Parallax Barrier and Lenticular Display. Parallax Barrier technology uses a barrier layer with precision slits to create the illusion of depth through separate views for each eye. Lenticular Display technology employs a series of lenticular lenses to direct light rays to different viewing angles, creating a 3D effect without the need for glasses. Both technologies are popular in the market for providing immersive 3D viewing experiences without the use of special glasses.</p></p>
