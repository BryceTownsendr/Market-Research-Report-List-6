<p><p>The coffee seed oil market is divided into two main types: cold-pressed oils and CO2/solvent extraction oils. Cold-pressed oils are extracted without heat, preserving the oil's natural compounds and flavor, making them preferable for cosmetic and culinary uses. Conversely, CO2 and solvent extraction oils utilize chemical processes or supercritical CO2 to maximize yield and purity, often resulting in a more refined product with a longer shelf life. Each type caters to different applications and consumer preferences.</p></p>
