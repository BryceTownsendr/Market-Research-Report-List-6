<p><p>HJT Battery Market Types refer to different variations of the Heterojunction with Intrinsic Thin layer (HIT) solar cell technology. N-Type market refers to the use of n-type silicon wafers in the manufacturing process, which offer higher efficiency and better performance in high temperatures. P-Type market, on the other hand, utilizes p-type silicon wafers and is known for its lower cost and easier manufacturing process. Both types have their own advantages and are used in different applications based on specific requirements and priorities in the solar industry.</p></p>
