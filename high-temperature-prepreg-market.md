<p><p>The high-temperature prepreg market features two primary processing types: autoclave and out-of-autoclave (OOA). Autoclave processing involves using high pressure and temperature in a controlled environment to cure composite materials, ensuring uniformity and optimal performance. In contrast, OOA processing relies on ambient pressure, utilizing techniques such as vacuum bagging or compression molding, which can reduce costs and processing times while still achieving robust material properties. Both methods cater to industries like aerospace and automotive, demanding advanced composite solutions.</p></p>
