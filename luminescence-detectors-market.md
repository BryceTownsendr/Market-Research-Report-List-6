<p><p>Luminescence detectors are devices used to measure light emissions from samples in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and environmental monitoring. Tube luminescence detectors are traditional instruments that utilize a photomultiplier tube to measure luminescent signals. On the other hand, microplate luminescence detectors are more modern and compact, designed for high-throughput screening applications. They use specialized plates with multiple wells to measure luminescence from multiple samples simultaneously. Both types of detectors play a crucial role in research and development in various fields.</p></p>
