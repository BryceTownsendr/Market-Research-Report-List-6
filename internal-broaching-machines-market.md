<p><ul><li>Vertical</li><li>Horizontal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Internal broaching machines can be categorized into two main types based on their orientation: vertical and horizontal. Vertical broaching machines have a vertically aligned broach tool, allowing for the downward motion of the cutting tool. This type is typically used for applications that require precision and consistency. On the other hand, horizontal broaching machines have a horizontally oriented broach tool, which moves horizontally to cut the workpiece. This type is often used for high-volume production and larger workpieces.</p></p>
