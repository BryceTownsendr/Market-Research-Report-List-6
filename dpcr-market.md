<p><p>The dPCR market consists of two main types: Droplet Digital PCR and Chip-based Digital PCR. Droplet Digital PCR involves partitioning samples into thousands of droplets, each containing a single target molecule, allowing for precise measurement of DNA or RNA. Chip-based Digital PCR, on the other hand, uses microfluidic chips to partition samples into individual reaction chambers for accurate quantification of nucleic acids. Both technologies offer highly sensitive and precise measurement of nucleic acids, making them valuable tools in research and diagnostics.</p></p>
