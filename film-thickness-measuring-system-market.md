<p><p>The Film Thickness Measuring System market primarily includes Thickness Monitors and Spectrum Ellipsometers. Thickness Monitors utilize techniques such as interferometry to accurately measure the thickness of thin films in various applications, ensuring precise control in manufacturing processes. On the other hand, Spectrum Ellipsometers measure film thickness by analyzing the change in polarization of light after reflecting off a surface, providing high sensitivity and versatility for complex materials.
