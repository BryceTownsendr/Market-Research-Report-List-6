<p><p>The automatic fruit picking machines market can be categorized into three types based on their methods of harvesting: shaking, cutting, and dragging. Shaking machines use vibration to dislodge the fruits from the trees, while cutting machines use sharp blades to cut the fruits off the branches. Dragging machines pull the fruits off the trees using a combination of hooks and belts. Each type of machine offers unique advantages and features to cater to different types of fruits and harvesting conditions.</p></p>
