<p><p>Cell isolation technology includes various methods such as centrifugation, flow cytometry, and cell electrophoresis. Centrifugation separates cells based on their density using high-speed spinning. Flow cytometry sorts cells based on their specific markers using a laser. Cell electrophoresis separates cells based on their charge. These methods are crucial for isolating specific cell types for research, diagnostics, and therapeutic applications. Each of these technologies has unique advantages and is selected based on the specific needs of the study or application.</p></p>
