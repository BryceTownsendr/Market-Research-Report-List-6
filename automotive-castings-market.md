<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive Castings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure Die Casting</li><li>Vacuum Die Casting</li><li>Squeeze Die Casting</li><li>Semi-Solid Die Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive castings market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Pressure Die Casting** utilizes high pressure to inject molten metal into molds, ensuring precision and fine surface finishes.</p><p>2. **Vacuum Die Casting** employs a vacuum to reduce air entrapment, enhancing product integrity and minimizing defects.</p><p>3. **Squeeze Die Casting** combines pressure and solidification, producing dense components with improved mechanical properties.</p><p>4. **Semi-Solid Die Casting** involves partially molten metal, resulting in complex shapes and superior dimensional accuracy, optimizing performance and reducing waste.</p></p>
