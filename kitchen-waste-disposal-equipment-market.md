<p><p>The kitchen waste disposal equipment market includes two primary types: dry type and wet type. Dry type systems focus on grinding and shredding compostable waste, resulting in a manageable size for composting or landfill. These systems are typically less complex and energy-efficient. In contrast, wet type systems involve the use of water to break down organic waste through methods like maceration, creating a slurry for sewage disposal or treatment. Both types serve to minimize kitchen waste and enhance sanitation.</p></p>
