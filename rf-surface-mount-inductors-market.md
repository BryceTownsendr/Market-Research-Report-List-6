<p><strong>The RF Surface Mount Inductors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wire Wound Type</li><li>Film Type</li><li>Multilayer Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RF surface mount inductors market can be categorized into three main types: wire wound, film, and multilayer inductors. Wire wound inductors utilize coiled wire to achieve higher inductance values, making them suitable for high-frequency applications. Film inductors employ a thin film of conductive material, providing excellent performance with low distortion and high Q factors. Multilayer inductors consist of multiple layers of conductive and insulating materials, enabling compact designs with effective performance in high-density layouts, ideal for modern electronic devices.</p></p>
