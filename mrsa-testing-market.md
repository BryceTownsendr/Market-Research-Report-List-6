<p><ul><li>Immunodiagnostics</li><li>Molecular Diagnostics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MRSA testing market comprises two main types: immunodiagnostics and molecular diagnostics. Immunodiagnostics utilize antigen-antibody interactions to detect MRSA, employing methods like enzyme-linked immunosorbent assays (ELISA) and rapid tests. In contrast, molecular diagnostics focus on identifying the genetic material of MRSA through techniques such as polymerase chain reaction (PCR). Both types are crucial for accurate and timely detection of MRSA infections, helping facilitate appropriate treatment and control measures in healthcare settings.</p></p>
