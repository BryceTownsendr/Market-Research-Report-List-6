<p><strong>The Compact Solid state LIDAR Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MEMS Based Scanning</li><li>Phased Array</li><li>Non-Scanning Flash</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The compact solid-state LiDAR market encompasses three main types: MEMS-based scanning, phased array, and non-scanning flash. MEMS-based scanning utilizes micro-electromechanical systems to direct laser beams, offering high precision and compact design. Phased array technology employs multiple lasers to create a 3D image without mechanical movement, enhancing speed and durability. Non-scanning flash systems emit broad-spectrum laser pulses, capturing 3D data in a single shot, making them ideal for scenarios requiring rapid acquisition and simplicity in design.</p></p>
