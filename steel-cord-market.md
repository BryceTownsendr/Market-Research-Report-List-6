<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Steel Cord Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normal Tensile (NT)</li><li>High Tensile (HT)</li><li>Super Tensile (ST)</li><li>Ultra Tensile (UT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steel cord market is categorized into four types based on tensile strength. Normal Tensile (NT) cords are designed for standard applications, offering adequate strength and flexibility. High Tensile (HT) cords provide enhanced strength for demanding applications, improving durability. Super Tensile (ST) cords offer even higher tensile strength, suitable for extreme conditions. Lastly, Ultra Tensile (UT) cords deliver maximum strength, often used in specialized, high-performance environments such as aircraft or heavy-duty tires, ensuring reliability and safety.</p></p>
