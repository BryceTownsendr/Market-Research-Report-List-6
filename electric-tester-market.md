<p><p>Electric testers are widely used in the testing and maintenance of electrical systems. There are two main types of electric testers in the market - contact-type and contactless. Contact-type testers require direct physical contact with the system or component being tested, providing accurate measurements but potentially posing a safety risk. On the other hand, contactless testers can detect electrical issues without direct contact, offering convenience and increased safety. Both types of testers have their own advantages and are suited for different applications.</p></p>
