<p><p>The actuator systems market comprises four primary types: </p><p>1. **Electric Actuators** utilize electrical energy to produce motion, offering high precision and responsiveness. </p><p>2. **Electromechanical Actuators** combine mechanical systems and electric motors, delivering efficient motion control across various applications. </p><p>3. **Electrohydraulic Actuators** integrate electrical components with hydraulic systems, providing powerful force in compact designs. </p><p>4. **Electrohydrostatic Actuators** use fluid pressure generated by electric pumps, allowing for high performance in challenging environments. Each type serves specific industrial and technological needs.</p></p>
