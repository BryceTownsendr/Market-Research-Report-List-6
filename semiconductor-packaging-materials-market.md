<p><p>The semiconductor packaging materials market comprises several key types, each serving distinct functions. Organic substrates are primarily used as the base for mounting semiconductor devices, offering excellent thermal and electrical performance. Lead frames provide structural support and facilitate electrical connections in discrete components. Bonding wires connect the semiconductor chip to the package, ensuring signal transmission. Other materials include ceramic substrates and adhesive films, which enhance reliability, protect against environmental factors, and enable the miniaturization of electronic devices.</p></p>
