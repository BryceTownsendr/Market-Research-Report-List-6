<p><p>Hard Chemical Mechanical Polishing (CMP) pads are specialized materials used in the semiconductor manufacturing process. These pads facilitate the planarization of wafers by providing a controlled surface for the removal of excess material through both chemical and mechanical means. This dual-action process is essential for achieving the desired flatness and surface finishing of wafers, which are crucial for the performance of electronic devices.</p><p>The Hard CMP Pad Market is witnessing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for advanced semiconductor devices and the miniaturization of electronic components. As technologies evolve, industries such as consumer electronics, telecommunications, and automotive are pushing for finer and more efficient manufacturing processes, leading to higher adoption rates of CMP technologies.</p><p>The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.90% during the forecast period.
