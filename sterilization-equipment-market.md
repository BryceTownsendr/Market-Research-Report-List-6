<p><p>Sterilization equipment market can be classified into two main types: high temperature sterilization and low temperature sterilization. High temperature sterilization involves using processes like autoclaving to kill microorganisms at temperatures above 100°C. On the other hand, low temperature sterilization methods such as ethylene oxide sterilization or hydrogen peroxide gas plasma sterilization are used at lower temperatures to sterilize heat-sensitive materials. Both types of sterilization equipment play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and efficacy of medical equipment and supplies.</p></p>
