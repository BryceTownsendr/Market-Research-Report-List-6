<p><p>Diesel locomotive engines are categorized into different types based on their power transmission systems. Diesel-mechanical locomotives use a mechanical transmission to transfer power from the engine to the wheels. Diesel-electric locomotives use electric generators to produce power and electric motors to drive the wheels. Diesel-hydraulic locomotives use hydraulic transmissions to transfer power. Diesel-steam locomotives use steam engines for power. Diesel-pneumatic locomotives use pneumatic systems for power transmission. Each type of diesel locomotive engine offers different advantages and capabilities for various applications.</p></p>
