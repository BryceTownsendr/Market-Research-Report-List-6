<p><p>Composite Fabrication Technology Market Types include Lay-up, Filament Winding, Injection Molding, Pultrusion, Compression Molding, RTM, and Others. Lay-up involves placing layers of reinforcing fibers and resin into a mold by hand. Filament Winding is the process of winding filaments onto a rotating mandrel. Injection Molding involves injecting resin into a mold. Pultrusion pulls fibers through a resin bath and into a shaping die. Compression Molding compresses fibers and resin in a mold. RTM injects resin into a closed mold. Others refer to various alternative fabrication methods.</p></p>
