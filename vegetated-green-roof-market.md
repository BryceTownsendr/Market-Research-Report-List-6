<p><p>Vegetated Green Roof Market Types include Extensive (Shallow Depth) Modules, Semi-Intensive (Intermediate Depth) Modules, and Intensive (Maximum Depth) Modules. Extensive modules are characterized by low maintenance and lightweight plants, suitable for roofs with limited load capacity. Semi-Intensive modules have a moderate depth and allow for a wider variety of plant species, requiring more maintenance than extensive green roofs. Intensive modules have the greatest depth and support a diverse range of plant life, resembling a traditional garden and requiring the most maintenance.</p></p>
