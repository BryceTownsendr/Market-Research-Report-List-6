<p><strong>The Powered Surgical Handpieces Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric-Powered</li><li>Battery-Powered</li><li>Pneumatic-Powered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The powered surgical handpieces market includes three primary types: electric-powered, battery-powered, and pneumatic-powered devices. Electric-powered handpieces utilize a direct electrical connection for consistent power and high-speed operation, often providing superior torque and precision. Battery-powered handpieces offer portability and freedom of movement, making them ideal for procedures in various settings, although they may have limited run times. Pneumatic-powered handpieces rely on compressed air, delivering lightweight and efficient performance, particularly in specific surgical applications requiring precision without electrical reliance.</p></p>
