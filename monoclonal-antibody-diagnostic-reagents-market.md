<p><p>The monoclonal antibody diagnostic reagents market includes several assay types. Enzyme-linked immunosorbent assays (ELISA) detect antigens or antibodies using enzyme-linked antibodies for signal amplification. Dot-immunogold filtration assays utilize gold-labeled antibodies for rapid detection. The double antigen sandwich chemiluminescence method measures the presence of specific antigens with high sensitivity through chemiluminescent reactions. Recombinant immunoblot assays combine recombinant proteins with traditional immunoblotting for precise identification and quantification of antibodies, enhancing specificity and accuracy in diagnostics.</p></p>
