<p><p>The fingerprint sensor market comprises three main types: capacitive, optical, and thermal sensors. Capacitive sensors use electrical signals to detect ridges and valleys on a fingerprint, offering high accuracy and durability. Optical sensors capture detailed images of fingerprints using light, but can be affected by environmental conditions. Thermal sensors detect the heat emitted by a finger, making them effective in various scenarios. Each type has unique advantages, catering to different applications and user preferences in security and authentication.</p></p>
