<p><p>Automotive laminating adhesives are available in various types including solventborne, solventless, waterborne, and others. Solventborne adhesives contain volatile organic compounds and are commonly used in automotive manufacturing. Solventless adhesives are free from volatile components and are known for their high strength and durability. Waterborne adhesives are environmentally friendly options that offer good adhesion properties. Other types of automotive laminating adhesives include UV-curable adhesives and hot melt adhesives, each with their own unique characteristics and applications in the automotive industry.</p></p>
