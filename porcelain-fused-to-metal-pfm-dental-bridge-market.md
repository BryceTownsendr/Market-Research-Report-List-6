<p><p>The Porcelain Fused to Metal (PFM) dental bridge market includes several types based on design and support structures. Traditional bridges consist of two crowns on adjacent teeth supporting a false tooth in between. Cantilever bridges are anchored on one side only, making them suitable for specific gaps. Maryland bridges utilize resin-bonded frameworks for minimal tooth alteration, while implant-supported bridges attach to dental implants, offering stability and durability. Each type addresses different patient needs and clinical situations.</p></p>
