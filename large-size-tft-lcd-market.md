<p><p>The large size TFT LCD market consists of various types including TN (Twisted Nematic), IPS (In-Plane Switching), AFFS (Advanced Fringe Field Switching), MVA (Multidomain Vertical Alignment), and others. TN panels offer fast response times and are commonly used in gaming monitors. IPS panels provide superior color accuracy and wide viewing angles, making them ideal for professional applications. AFFS panels offer improved visibility in bright environments. MVA panels provide high contrast ratios and deep blacks. Other types include PLS (Plane to Line Switching) and AHVA (Advanced Hyper Viewing Angle) panels.</p></p>
