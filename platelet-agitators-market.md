<p><p>Platelet agitators are used to keep platelet concentrates agitated and prevent clumping. There are three main types of platelet agitators on the market: flatbed agitators, combination devices, and circular agitators. Flatbed agitators have a large surface area which allows for efficient agitation. Combination devices can be used for both agitating and storing platelets. Circular agitators have a rotating motion which helps to keep platelets suspended in a homogeneous manner. Each type of agitator serves a specific purpose in the storage and maintenance of platelet concentrates.</p></p>
