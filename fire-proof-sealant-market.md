<p><p>Fire-proof sealants are used in buildings to prevent the spread of fire. Elastomeric type sealants are made of flexible materials that can withstand movement and temperature changes, providing a durable fire barrier. Intumescent type sealants expand when exposed to heat, forming a protective char layer that insulates the substrate from fire. Both types of sealants play a crucial role in enhancing fire safety in buildings by containing fires and delaying their spread, ultimately saving lives and property.</p></p>
