<p><strong>The Anti Fog Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydrophilic Anti-fog</li><li>Hydrophobic Anti-fog</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Anti-Fog Paint market is primarily categorized into two types: hydrophilic and hydrophobic anti-fog paints. Hydrophilic anti-fog paints attract moisture, spreading it into a thin layer that prevents fogging on surfaces, making them ideal for environments where visibility is crucial. In contrast, hydrophobic anti-fog paints repel water, creating a barrier that prevents condensation, suitable for applications requiring robust protection against fogging. Both types serve distinct needs across industries such as automotive, safety goggles, and eyewear.</p></p>
