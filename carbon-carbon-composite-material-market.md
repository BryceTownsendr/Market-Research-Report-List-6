<p><strong>The Carbon-Carbon Composite Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chemical Vapor Deposition</li><li>Liquid Impregnation Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon-carbon composite material market primarily utilizes two production methods: Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) and Liquid Impregnation Process. CVD involves depositing carbon-rich gases onto a substrate, leading to a dense and high-performance material ideal for high-temperature applications. In contrast, the Liquid Impregnation Process involves saturating a carbon fiber preform with a resin or pitch, which is then carbonized to create a lightweight, strong composite. Both methods cater to industries like aerospace, automotive, and defense, emphasizing performance and thermal stability.</p></p>
