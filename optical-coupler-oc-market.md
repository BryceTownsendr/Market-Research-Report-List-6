<p><p>The Optical Coupler (OC) market can be categorized into two main types: linear and non-linear optical couplers. Linear optical couplers maintain a proportional relationship between input and output signals, making them ideal for applications requiring stable signal transmission. In contrast, non-linear optical couplers enable signal processing through interactions that vary with intensity, facilitating advanced functionalities such as signal amplification or wavelength conversion. These differing characteristics allow each type to serve unique needs in telecommunications, data communication, and sensor applications.</p></p>
