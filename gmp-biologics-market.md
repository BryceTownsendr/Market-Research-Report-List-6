<p><p>The GMP biologics market encompasses various types of antibody products, notably monoclonal antibodies and polyclonal antibodies. Monoclonal antibodies are produced from a single clone of immune cells, ensuring uniformity and specificity, making them ideal for targeted therapies. In contrast, polyclonal antibodies are derived from multiple immune cell lines, offering a diverse range of binding capabilities, which can enhance effectiveness against certain conditions. Both types play crucial roles in diagnostics and therapeutics, catering to a growing demand for biologic treatments.</p></p>
