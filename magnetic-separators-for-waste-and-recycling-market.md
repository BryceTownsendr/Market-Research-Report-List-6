<p><p>Magnetic separators for waste and recycling come in various types that enhance material recovery. Eddy current separators use magnetic fields to separate non-ferrous metals from other materials. Overband magnetic separators are designed to remove ferrous contaminants from bulk materials on conveyor belts. Drum type magnetic separators efficiently separate magnetic materials from non-magnetic substances through rotating drums. Other types may include magnetic pulleys or plate separators, each tailored for specific waste processing needs to improve recycling efficiency and material purity.</p></p>
