<p><p>Electric sump pumps are categorized into two main types: submersible pumps and pedestal pumps. Submersible pumps are designed to be placed directly in the sump pit and can handle higher volumes of water. Pedestal pumps, on the other hand, have the motor located above the sump pit and are more suitable for smaller spaces. Both types of pumps are electrically powered and are commonly used to remove water from basements or other enclosed spaces to prevent flooding and water damage.</p></p>
