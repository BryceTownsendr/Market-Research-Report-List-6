<p><ul><li>Pulse</li><li>Analog</li><li>Network</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The servo drive system market can be categorized into three types: pulse, analog, and network. Pulse systems utilize digital signals to control motor position and speed with high precision, making them ideal for applications requiring quick responses. Analog systems rely on continuous signals for control, offering smooth operation for less demanding tasks. Networked systems integrate multiple drives and controllers over industrial networks, facilitating real-time communication and centralized monitoring, thus enhancing automation and system performance across various applications.</p></p>
