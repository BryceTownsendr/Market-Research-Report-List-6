<p><ul><li>Double-Acting Hydraulic Cylinders</li><li>Single-Acting Hydraulic Cylinders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electro-hydraulic cylinders market comprises primarily two types: double-acting and single-acting hydraulic cylinders. Double-acting hydraulic cylinders utilize hydraulic pressure in both extending and retracting strokes, providing greater control and force for demanding applications. In contrast, single-acting hydraulic cylinders use hydraulic force to extend, relying on gravity or springs for retraction, making them simpler and often more cost-effective. Both types find applications in diverse industries, including manufacturing, construction, and automotive, catering to varying operational requirements.</p></p>
