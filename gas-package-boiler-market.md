D-type boilers have a vertical layout, providing efficient steam generation in limited spaces. A-type boilers offer a horizontal design, allowing for easy maintenance and operational flexibility. O-type boilers, with their circular configuration, are suited for high-capacity applications, ensuring balanced heat distribution.
