<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The MRAM Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Toggle MRAM</li><li>STT-MRAM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MRAM market comprises two main types: Toggle MRAM and STT-MRAM. Toggle MRAM utilizes magnetic field-induced switching to change states, offering fast read and write speeds, but faces challenges in scalability. In contrast, STT-MRAM (Spin-Transfer Torque MRAM) leverages spin-polarized currents for data writing, enabling higher density storage and better scalability. Both technologies provide non-volatile memory solutions, enhancing performance for applications in computing, automotive, and consumer electronics, while competing in terms of price, performance, and energy efficiency.</p></p>
