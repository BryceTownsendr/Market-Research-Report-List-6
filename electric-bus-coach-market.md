<p><p>The electric bus and coach market comprises several types, each with distinct technologies. Full Battery Electric Buses (BEB) rely entirely on rechargeable batteries for propulsion, promoting zero emissions. Hybrid Electric Buses (HEB) combine conventional internal combustion engines with electric propulsion, enhancing fuel efficiency and reducing emissions. Fuel Cell Electric Buses (FCEB) use hydrogen fuel cells to generate electricity, offering longer ranges and quick refueling compared to BEBs. Each type contributes to sustainable urban transportation solutions, reducing reliance on fossil fuels.</p></p>
