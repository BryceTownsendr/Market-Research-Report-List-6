<p><strong>The Marine Batteries Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gel Battery</li><li>Absorbed Glass Mat (AGM) Battery</li><li>Flooded Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine batteries come in various types, including Gel Battery, Absorbed Glass Mat (AGM) Battery, and Flooded Battery. Gel batteries use silica to store electrolytes and are sealed, making them maintenance-free and resistant to vibration. AGM batteries have fiberglass mats between the plates to hold the electrolytes, providing higher starting power. Flooded batteries have removable caps for adding distilled water and are cost-effective but require regular maintenance. Each type has its own advantages and is suitable for different marine applications.</p></p>
