<p><p>The non-contact multi-function infrared thermometer market comprises mainly two types: forehead type and ear type thermometers. Forehead thermometers utilize infrared technology to measure body temperature from a distance without contact, ensuring quick and hygienic readings, commonly used in homes and medical facilities. Ear thermometers, on the other hand, require placement in the ear canal to provide accurate temperature readings, ideal for infants and young children. Both types emphasize convenience and efficiency in temperature monitoring, especially during health screenings.</p></p>
