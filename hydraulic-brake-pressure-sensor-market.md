<p><p>The Hydraulic Brake Pressure Sensor market is segmented into two main types based on output: Analog Output and Digital Output. Analog Output sensors provide a continuous voltage signal that correlates with the brake pressure, allowing for real-time monitoring and feedback in braking systems. In contrast, Digital Output sensors deliver discrete signals, typically in binary form, which can enhance compatibility with modern electronic control units. Both types play crucial roles in improving vehicle safety, responsiveness, and overall driving experience.</p></p>
