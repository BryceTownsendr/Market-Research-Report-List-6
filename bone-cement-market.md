<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Bone Cement Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Viscosity Cements</li><li>Medium Viscosity Cements</li><li>High Viscosity Cements</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bone cement market is categorized into three types based on viscosity: low, medium, and high viscosity cements. Low viscosity cements are primarily used for enhanced flow and penetration in complex fractures, ensuring stability in osteoporotic bones. Medium viscosity cements provide a balance between flow and mechanical strength, making them suitable for a variety of orthopedic applications. High viscosity cements offer excellent mechanical properties and maintain shape, often used in load-bearing areas and for spinal applications, providing robust fixation.</p></p>
