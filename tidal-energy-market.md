<p><p>The tidal energy market can be categorized into four main types: Tidal Stream Generator, Pendulum Device, Barrage, and Others. Tidal Stream Generators harness the kinetic energy of moving water to generate electricity. Pendulum Devices use the motion of tides to move a pendulum and generate energy. Barrages involve building a dam-like structure to capture and release tidal energy. Other methods include turbines, platforms, and tidal lagoons. Each type has its own unique approach to harnessing tidal power for electricity generation.</p></p>
