<p><p>A spiral escalator is a type of escalator that follows a curved path instead of a straight one. There are two main types of spiral escalator markets: Isokinetic Operation and Frequency Conversion. Isokinetic Operation escalators maintain a constant speed regardless of the number of users on the escalator, while Frequency Conversion escalators adjust their speed based on the number of users, leading to energy savings and improved efficiency. Both types offer unique benefits and cater to different customer needs.</p></p>
