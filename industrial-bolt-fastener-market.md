<p><p>The industrial bolt market features various types, including half screw bolts and full screw bolts. Half screw bolts, characterized by a partially threaded shaft, provide improved grip on assembly without the need for complete threaded engagement. In contrast, full screw bolts have a fully threaded body, ensuring a secure connection suitable for high-stress applications. Both types cater to different industrial needs, with half screw bolts often used in softer materials and full screw bolts preferred for robust structural requirements.</p></p>
