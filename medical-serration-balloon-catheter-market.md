<p><strong>The Medical Serration Balloon Catheter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>OTW Serration Balloon Catheter</li><li>Rapid Exchange Serration Balloon Catheter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical serration balloon catheter market is segmented into two main types: over-the-wire (OTW) serration balloon catheters and rapid exchange serration balloon catheters. OTW serration balloon catheters involve a guiding wire that runs through the catheter, allowing for precise placement and flexibility in navigating complex vascular structures. In contrast, rapid exchange serration balloon catheters feature a design that enables quicker access and exchange of devices, improving procedural efficiency and reducing time under sedation during interventions.</p></p>
