<p><p>Temporary cardiac pacing leads are essential in managing patients with transient heart rhythm issues. Unipolar leads utilize a single electrode to deliver electrical impulses to the heart, providing a simpler setup and ease of use. In contrast, bipolar leads feature two electrodes that work together, reducing the risk of interference and improving signal clarity. The unipolar market generally sees wider adoption due to its simplicity, while the bipolar market is favored for complex cases requiring more precise pacing capabilities.</p></p>
