<p><p>The tie-down strap market features three main types: Cam Straps, Over-Center Lever Cam Style Straps, and Ratchet Straps. Cam Straps utilize a simple cam mechanism to secure loads with ease, ideal for light-to-moderate applications. Over-Center Lever Cam Style Straps provide a more robust locking mechanism for heavier loads, offering increased tension. Ratchet Straps are designed for maximum tightening and security, making them suitable for high-stress transport situations. Each type serves specific needs in load securing.</p></p>
