<p><ul><li>Freeze Recovery</li><li>Wheel Recovery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The NMP Recycling System market can be categorized primarily into two types: Freeze Recovery and Wheel Recovery. Freeze Recovery involves lowering the temperature of NMP to solidify contaminants, allowing for easy separation and collection, which enhances purity levels. In contrast, Wheel Recovery employs mechanical methods for regeneration through rotating drums or wheels, optimizing separation efficiency and minimizing waste. Both methods aim to promote sustainability in the recycling of NMP, a solvent widely used in battery production and electronics manufacturing.</p></p>
