<p><p>Automotive glass for the aftermarket can be categorized into three main types: laminated glass, tempered glass, and other specialized types. Laminated glass is made by sandwiching a layer of polyvinyl butyral between two layers of glass, providing strength and safety by holding the glass together when shattered. Tempered glass is heat-treated to increase its strength and break into small, dull pieces when shattered. Other market types include heated glass, acoustic glass, and privacy glass, each offering unique features and benefits for different automotive applications.</p></p>
