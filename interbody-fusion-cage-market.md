<p><p>The interbody fusion cage market is categorized into four main types based on spinal regions: lumbar, cervical, thoracolumbar, and thoracic. Lumbar cages are designed for the lower back, addressing conditions like herniated discs. Cervical cages are used in the neck area for similar issues. Thoracolumbar cages serve the junction of the thoracic and lumbar spine, while thoracic cages are utilized for the upper and mid-back. Each type is tailored to specific anatomical requirements and surgical protocols to enhance spinal stability and healing.</p></p>
