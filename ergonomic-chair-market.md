<p><p>Ergonomic chairs with 2-3 degrees of freedom adjustment offer basic features such as seat height adjustment and backrest tilt. These chairs are suitable for general office use and provide some level of customization for user comfort. On the other hand, ergonomic chairs with above 3 degrees of freedom adjustment offer advanced features like adjustable armrests, lumbar support, and headrest. These chairs cater to specific needs of users who require more individualized support and comfort, such as those with back or neck issues or those who spend long hours sitting.</p></p>
