<p><p>LED Light Engine Market is divided into two main types: Flexible and Rigid. </p><p>Flexible LED light engines are designed to bend and conform to different shapes and provide versatile lighting solutions for various applications. They are often used in signage, automotive lighting, and decorative lighting.</p><p>Rigid LED light engines, on the other hand, are more structured and are commonly used in general lighting applications such as downlights, troffers, and high bays. They offer a more straightforward installation process and are suitable for more traditional lighting fixtures.</p></p>
