<p><p>Laser Beam Deflection Systems Market is segmented into three types: Acousto-optic Deflectors, Electro-Optic Deflectors, and Others. Acousto-optic deflectors use sound waves to change the refractive index of a material, deflecting the laser beam. Electro-optic deflectors use an electric field to change the optical properties of a crystal, thereby altering the beam's path. Other types of deflectors may include MEMS-based systems or holographic deflectors. Each of these systems offers unique benefits and capabilities for various applications in laser technology.</p></p>
