<p><p>The hand push trencher market includes two main types: wheel trenchers and chain trenchers. Wheel trenchers use a rotating wheel with digging teeth to cut through the ground, making them suitable for softer soil types. Chain trenchers, on the other hand, use a chain with cutting teeth to dig into harder ground and rocky terrain. Each type has its own advantages and is used in different applications based on the soil conditions and depth of the trench needed.</p></p>
