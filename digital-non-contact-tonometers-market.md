<p><p>Digital Non-contact Tonometers (NCTs) are advanced diagnostic devices used to measure intraocular pressure (IOP) without direct contact with the eye. These instruments utilize a puff of air to flatten the cornea, allowing for accurate pressure readings, which are crucial in diagnosing conditions like glaucoma. The primary advantages of NCTs include their quick, painless operation and reduced risk of infection compared to traditional tonometry methods.</p><p>The Digital Non-contact Tonometers Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8% during the forecast period.
