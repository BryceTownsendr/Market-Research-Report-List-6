<p><p>The aviation refuelling equipment market includes various types of equipment such as hoses, couplings, nozzles, expansion joints, fuel trucks, and others. Hoses are used to transfer fuel from the storage tank to the aircraft, while couplings ensure a secure connection between different components. Nozzles control the flow of fuel during refuelling, and expansion joints provide flexibility in the system. Fuel trucks are mobile units used for refuelling aircraft on the tarmac. Other equipment may include filters, meters, and control valves for efficient and safe refuelling operations.</p></p>
