CMP facilitates seamless connectivity for IoT devices across different networks, ensuring reliable communication. AEP focuses on providing tools for developers to build and manage IoT applications, enabling integration and scalability. PES enhances interoperability by allowing various IoT devices to communicate using different protocols, thus ensuring a cohesive IoT ecosystem and improving device management across diverse platforms.</p></p>
